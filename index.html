Modular Housing
Sectional, portable housing suitable for those who do not have homes.

Many homeless and refugees do not have access to housing and shelter due to overpopulation, lack of government programs, and border controls.

Volunteer
There are over 600,000 homeless people in the United States and about 9 million Syrian refugees.

Offer to take in a homeless person/refugee for a temporary amount of time. You will be able to provide shelter for a person in need and guide them to the next step in life.

Donate
A donation even as small as a dollar can mean a lot when it adds up to thousands and even millions.
