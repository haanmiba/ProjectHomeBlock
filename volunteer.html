Project HomeBlock

Volunteer to Host a Homeless Person/Refugee

Any refugee and homeless person going through Project HomeBlock is logged into and investigated in our database. Their crime history, drug history, and past has been documented and we ensure that only those who are classified as safe and stable are the ones that will be matched to volunteers to host them.
